## Per-field rights

Each verse record mixes content with different rights status:

| Field | Source / status |
|-------|-----------------|
| `devanagari` | Mūla (root) Sanskrit text — **public domain** |
| `english_devnagari` | IAST transliteration — mechanical derivative, no separate claim |
| `verse_Syn` (morphology) | Grammatical analysis aggregated from wisdomlib.org's "Analysis of Sanskrit grammar" sections |
| `translation` | **Copyrighted** — from a published English edition (see below) |
| `purport` | **Copyrighted** — editorial commentary / footnotes from the same published edition |
